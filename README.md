### 🔍 Key Market Insights (Sample Run Analysis)
* **Ecosystem Concentration:** The top-10 crypto market cap is heavily consolidated, with Bitcoin and Ethereum commanding ~74.6% of the overall network value. 
* **Volatility vs. Scale:** The 4D liquidity analysis confirmed classical financial distributions; high-market-cap foundation assets exhibited tight, low-variance daily pricing bands, while lower-tier market-cap entries showed maximum positive price variance (+11.5% to +12.8%) on low relative volume profiles.
